POUR DECISIONS

Pick a meal and a drink

Pour Decisions is an app that takes some of the time out of finding a recipe for dinner and drinks and gives it back to you. It lets the user choose a random meal and a random drink from https://www.themealdb.com/api and https://www.thecocktaildb.com/api. If the user likes the food or drink, they can store the search. The food and drink divs have dropdowns to show the ingrediants and recipe. Javascript JQUERY Bulma HTML CSS
